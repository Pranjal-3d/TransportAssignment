# Job Description: Senior Python Developer

## About the Role
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Python Developer to join our core architecture team. You will be responsible for designing and implementing scalable backend services, optimizing system performance, and mentoring junior developers.

## Key Requirements
- **Experience:** Minimum 8 years of professional software development experience, primarily in Python.
- **Skills:** Expert knowledge of Python 3.x, FastAPI/Flask, and asynchronous programming.
- **Cloud:** Strong experience with AWS (Lambda, ECS, RDS) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
- **Database:** Proficient in PostgreSQL and Redis.
- **Education:** Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
- **Domain:** Experience in FinTech or High-Traffic E-commerce systems is a plus.

## Responsibilities
- Architect and develop high-performance microservices.
- Design resilient distributed systems.
- Conduct code reviews and ensure engineering excellence.
- Collaborate with product managers to define technical roadmaps.
